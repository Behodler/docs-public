# The Phoenix Roadmap  

Phoenix is built to be a **self-strengthening system**, built to grow more resilient, more liquid, and more valuable over time.  

Its **Evergreen architecture** ensures that every phase of development compounds into the next — transforming volatility into stability, yield into growth, and participation into ownership.  

This roadmap charts each phase of Phoenix roadmap and then situates Phoenix within the broader context of the Behodler Ecosystem.

---

## Phase 1: Ignition 

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b31f5fa4-9a62-45ad-a51b-a4b1e43d4e8e" width="55%">

Problem: How do you launch a stablecoin that’s strong from day one?

As noted in our introduction to the concept of an Evergreen Stablecoin current stablecoins come with trade-offs:

Some are centralised, some are capital-inefficient, and some are vulnerable to death-spiral de-peg events. 

This poses a challenge for aspiring decentralised stablecoins coming to market: How to gain a foothold? Often the fallback approach is to rely on emissions to incentivise liquidity provision. It has been a challenge for DeFi to shake the tendency of an inflationary mindset. Such an approach would not do for Behodler, who has always been animated by a deflationary philosophy.

Phoenix solves this through an **ignition** — a *controlled capitalization phase* that ensures every token minted is underpinned by real, compounding yield.  

At launch, users acquire **phUSD** using the **Behodler 3 bonding curve**, the first demonstration of Behodler's upgraded AMM tech. Through this purchase of phUSD real external yield that grows automatically over time is acquired by Phoenix.

This design achieves three critical goals:
1. **Guaranteed collateral growth** – Every dollar that enters Phoenix begins earning immediately.  
2. **Aligned incentives** – Early adopters, whales, and arbitrageurs all benefit from participation that strengthens the system rather than draining it.  
3. **Structured liquidity formation** – As demand for phUSD rises along the bonding curve, price discovery happens gradually, eliminating the chaos of open-market launches and ensuring that all participants will be aligned for phase 2.

By the time the bonding curve reaches $1, Phoenix has accumulated deep, yield-bearing backing and a healthy liquidity foundation. The protocol can then safely transition to fixed-price minting, having acquired the base from which it's collateral base grows organically.  

It solves the bootstrapping problem by fusing liquidity generation and yield production from the outset.

With a strong capital base in place, Phoenix is ready to stabilize — and the Evergreen cycle begins.

## Phase 2: Stabilisation 

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fe047e99-f427-4fd9-94c8-d5f8a7fcc53a" width="55%">

In the **Stabilisation phase**, phUSD finds its equilibrium through the Evergreen Loop we have already covered elsewhere. To recap:

- When phUSD trades **above $1**, arbitrageurs mint new phUSD at the fixed $1 price.  
- They sell that phUSD into the premium, expanding both the **liquidity depth in the LP** and the **yield vault TVL**.  
- When phUSD trades **below $1**, the yield vault buys back phUSD, rebounding back to peg.  

Each arbitrage cycle **increases TVL**, deepens liquidity, and compounds the underlying yield.  The protocol owned liquidity in the yield vault grows over time and Phoenix becomes stronger.

Phase 2 creates a **perpetual motion machine for stability**, laying the groundwork for the next phase: yield distribution.

## Phase 3: Yield

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f4826a43-b55d-4fa7-952d-896700977f09" width="55%">

How do you create yield in DeFi that is sustainable and compounding, not fleeting?

DeFi’s yield problem is simple: most returns come from emissions, and emissions eventually stop/inevitably fall in value. Phoenix solves this by making **yield the collateral itself**.  

In **Phase 3**, holders can **stake phUSD** to mint **sphUSD**, a thriftcoin that steadily rises in value as it accrues yield from Phoenix’s yield vault.  

sphUSD cannot fall in value relative to phUSD — it can only rise.  
This makes it a true *store of growing value* within the Phoenix ecosystem — a thriftcoin in the literal sense.  

Every phase before this one was preparation:
- Phase 1 built the capital base.  
- Phase 2 locked the peg and ensured flow of yield.  
- Phase 3 delivers that yield directly to holders.

## Phase 4: Wield

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/34692d69-2e50-402a-a293-057f57b4bbbd" width="60%">

How does Phoenix turn stability into influence?

"With great TVL comes great responsibility" - Uncle Justin, maybe.

By this stage, phUSD is stable, sphUSD is compounding, and liquidity is deep. The next step is to **put that power to work** — not just within Phoenix, but across the broader DeFi landscape.  

Key utilities come online:

- **Burning legacy tokens** – phUSD becomes a deflationary force within the Behodler ecosystem, using yield to recover Flax and SCX2.  
- **Charging Limbo** – A portion of yield generated by Phoenix will be used to incentivise liquidity provision on Limbo.  
- **Ecosystem integrations** – possibilities are opened up for phUSD and sphUSD to integrate directly into DeFi, such as serving as collateral in lending markets, yield assets in derivative platforms, and components in structured and hedging products.

At this stage, Phoenix transforms from a vault into a **monetary engine** — its internally generated, protocol-owned yield makes it attractive for external integrations. At the same time, yield from Phoenix strengthens the Behodler Ecosystem by bringing deflationary forces.

## Phase 5: Guild

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/01812b18-eaf6-40ec-9254-3735c4dde383" width="60%">

To keep the system adaptable and decentralised, control over how yield is routed needs to move gradually from the team to the community.  

The **Guild phase** introduces this next layer of decentralisation through **EYE staking**.  
EYE stakers gain influence over the **flow of yield** — deciding how and where Phoenix directs its internally generated rewards.  

EYE holders become the custodians of Phoenix’s ongoing growth, ensuring yield is allocated in ways that reinforce stability and align with community priorities.

## Phase 6: Shield

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a44dc285-ba9f-4015-8d6f-dcdb52ca07d3" width="40%">

Key to Phoenix's Evergreen promise,  is not just growth, but sustainability. 

The **Shield phase**:

- Introduces **multi-vault diversification** and **risk protection mechanisms**.  
- Spreads capital across **multiple yield vaults** to eliminate single points of failure.  

With Shield active, Phoenix achieves true permanence.  
phUSD remains stable under stress; sphUSD has a permanent growing base; and the entire ecosystem becomes resilient to external shocks.  

At this point, Phoenix stands as a **complete, self-sustaining system** — one where stability, yield, and security all reinforce each other.
